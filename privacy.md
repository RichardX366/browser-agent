# Privacy Policy for Browser Agent

Effective date: 4/27/2026

Browser Agent is a Chrome extension that provides an AI-powered side panel for helping users inspect and interact with web pages.

## Data Collection

Browser Agent does not collect, sell, or store user browsing data on our servers. We do not operate a backend service for collecting user information.

The extension runs locally in your browser. Information may be processed locally only as needed to perform user-requested actions, such as inspecting a page, clicking, typing, scrolling, navigating tabs, or summarizing visible content.

## Information Processed Locally

Depending on how you use the extension, Browser Agent may locally process:

- Website content from pages you ask the agent to inspect or interact with
- Page titles, URLs, and tab information needed to perform browser actions
- User prompts and chat messages entered into the extension
- Tool activity such as clicks, scrolling, typing, tab navigation, and page inspection results
- Console and network request information when you ask the agent to inspect or debug a page
- Provider settings and preferences

This information is not collected by us or stored on our servers.

## Local Browser Storage

Browser Agent may store limited information locally in your browser to make the extension work across sessions, such as:

- API keys you enter
- Provider and model settings
- Extension preferences
- Local conversation history

This data stays in your browser’s local extension storage. We do not receive it, collect it, or store it on external servers.

## AI Providers

Browser Agent lets you connect to third-party AI providers, such as OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, Mistral, and Moonshot/Kimi.

When you use the agent, the extension may send the information needed to complete your request to the AI provider you selected. This may include your prompt, relevant conversation context, page content, tool outputs, and page state. This transmission happens directly from your browser to the selected provider’s API.

We do not receive, collect, or store the information sent to your selected AI provider. The selected provider’s handling of that data is governed by its own terms and privacy policy.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell user data.

We do not transfer user data to third parties except when the extension sends information directly to the AI provider selected by the user for the purpose of completing the user’s request.

We do not use or transfer user data for advertising, creditworthiness, lending, or unrelated purposes.

## User Control

You control what tasks you ask Browser Agent to perform and which AI provider you connect.

You can clear local conversation history and extension data through the extension interface or Chrome’s extension storage controls.

You should avoid asking the extension to process sensitive information unless you are comfortable sending that information to your selected AI provider.

## Security

Browser Agent uses Chrome extension APIs and browser storage to operate. API keys and settings are stored locally in your browser. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, so users should use care when entering sensitive information.

## Children’s Privacy

Browser Agent is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.
